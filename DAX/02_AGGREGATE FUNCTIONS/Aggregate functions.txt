AVERAGE : TAKES AVERAGE OF NUMERICS
AVERAGEA : TAKE AVERAGE OF NUMERICS...
		CONSIDER TRUE =1,FALSE AND OTHER NON-NUMERIC = 0
AVERAGEX : ROW GET ADD AND OVERALL AVG IS TAKEN

COUNT : COUNT RECORDS ;SKIPS BLANKS AND TRUE/FALSE
COUNTA : INCLUDES TRUE/FALSE

MAX : MAXIMUM IN A COLUMN,INCLUDE STRINGS/NUMERICS
	TRUE/FALSE NOT SUPPORTED

MIN : MINIMUM IN A COLUMN, INCLUDE NUMBERS/TEXT/DATES/BLANKS
	TRUE/FALSE NOT SUPPORTED
SUM : ADDITION IN COL LEVEL
SUMX: ADDITION IN ROW LEVEL

PRODUCT: PRODUCT OF COL LEVEL
PRODUCTX : PRODUCT AT ROW LEVEL
